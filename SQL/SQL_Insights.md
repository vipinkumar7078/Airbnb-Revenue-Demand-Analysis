# Airbnb Revenue & Demand Analysis - SQL Insights

## Project Objective

Analyze Airbnb listings using SQL to identify pricing trends, booking demand, host performance, and market opportunities.

---

# Key Business Insights

## 1. Listing Distribution

- The dataset contains thousands of Airbnb listings across multiple cities.
- Listings are concentrated in a few high-demand markets.

---

## 2. Pricing Analysis

- Significant price variation exists across cities.
- Premium cities command much higher average prices.
- Luxury listings create price outliers.

---

## 3. Room Type Analysis

- Entire Home/Apartment is the most common room type.
- Private Rooms are the second largest category.
- Hotel Rooms have the fewest listings.

---

## 4. Host Analysis

- A small number of hosts manage a large share of listings.
- Professional hosts contribute significantly to the Airbnb market.

---

## 5. Booking Demand

- Listings with higher reviews generally indicate higher booking demand.
- Reviews from the last 12 months help identify active listings.

---

## 6. Availability Analysis

- Many listings have low yearly availability, suggesting strong occupancy.
- Highly available listings may indicate lower demand.

---

## 7. Revenue Opportunities

- Premium locations generate higher potential revenue.
- Optimizing pricing in high-demand cities can increase earnings.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Implement dynamic pricing based on city demand.
- Promote high-performing listings to maximize occupancy.
- Improve visibility for low-review listings.
- Encourage hosts to maintain competitive pricing.
- Focus marketing efforts on cities with strong demand.
